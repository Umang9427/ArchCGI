Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Process to generate the Images (Renders) shown in this website?

ArchCGI's Workflow: Concept Research/ Planning Overview-> Modelling -> Texturing -> Environment/Lighting -> Draft Renders -> Minor changes -> Final Rendering -> Post Production.

What is the Time taken per Rendering Project?


ArchCGI's Timeline: The estimated time for a project depends on the scale of it. Our standard estimate for a project is 4 days: 2 days for concept/ modelling and 2 days for rendering. If you are in a hurry, we can provide a quick 3D concept plan in 1 day.

How Do you Communicate with us?


We provide ease of accessibility to our customers regarding communication. We use more than 10 video conference providers and we acknowledge social media conversation. Our goal is to regularly update the clients about the progress of the work.

How much does it cost? Do you charge per render?


Our services for 3D Exterior, Interior and Landscape start from 100$. You can get the detailed quotation of our price through quick contact button in our webpage or by sending us an Email from Contact Us page. Usually, we do not charge per render.
